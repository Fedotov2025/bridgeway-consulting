Why We

We are a young team of people from post-Soviet countries who have gained experience in German companies in the fields of marketing, financial auditing and consulting. In our pilot project BridgeWay we help entrepreneurs to find a niche for their product in the German market. Interested? Great, it costs you nothing! Take a look at the services we provide and sign up for a free initial consultation.

What We Do

  • Market Research

    We will conduct market analysis for your product, whether it's B2B, B2C, e-commerce, or retail.

  • Market Entry

    We will create a detailed plan for launching your product in the German market, including customer acquisition, finding optimal logistics routes, and licensing your product in Germany.

  • Business Plan

    We will prepare an initial business plan taking into account the specifics of German taxation, legalization costs, and supply routes.

  • Incorporation

    We will write a concise and clear guide on legalizing your product in Germany, covering licensing, taxation, and registration in commercial registries.

  • Company Representation

    We will conduct negotiations with interested clients on your behalf.
